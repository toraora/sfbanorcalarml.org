### What is ARML Local?

ARML Local is a "mini-ARML" competition written by the official ARML organizers that is run
regionally across the country. Teams and individuals are ranked across all
regions, and there are cash prizes for top teams and the top individual. Winners
will also be recognized at the awards ceremony in Reno on May 30.

It's a great way to get a feel for what ARML will be like, get some practice,
and meet potential teammates. ARML Local is free to attend, and you're welcome
to participate even if you have no intention of going to Reno with the team.

For information about the competition, you can review the
[ARML Local Competitor
Instructions](https://docs.google.com/document/d/13cjIKZnKJ4sSFFN5GQiMmi_aHoRGiYq_xozj_OQcrl8/).

### How do I participate?

We are hosting ARML Local in two times and places:
- *Bay Area:* Sunday, 4/19 @ 9am-1pm, [Bliss and Wisdom Foundation, San Jose](https://maps.app.goo.gl/n1exw4G9n4uY2VgVA)
- *Sacramento Area:* Sunday, 4/19 @ 12pm-3pm, [UC Davis Shields Library Room 162](https://maps.app.goo.gl/xyWfHHhvpzjJwrmY8)

There is no need to register in advance, though we do ask that you come on-time.

### How are teams formed?

Please be prompt as we'll need to form teams on the spot for ARML Local. We'll
ask everyone to sign in upon arrival and coordinate team formation. If you have
friends you'd like to be on a team with, you can indicate that when you sign in.

### What if I have a time conflict?

You don't have to participate in ARML Local in order to compete with us in Reno
at ARML. We do treat ARML Local as a tryout opportunity for elite team
selection, but we will have other tryout opportunities, and you're not required
to attend every tryout. All registered students are welcome to join the team
regardless of tryout participation. For more information about tryouts, see our
[Practices](/practices/) page.

### What can parents do during ARML Local?

For those of you coming to San Jose, you're not required to stick around,
though we may be able to set out some tables/chairs if you need a place to stay.
We'll also be available to answer any questions that you have about ARML.

In order for us to be most efficient, we could also use a few volunteers to help
us distribute test papers and proctor the competition. If you're available to
help, please let us know when you arrive, and we'd really appreciate your help.
