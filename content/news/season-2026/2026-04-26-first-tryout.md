Dear ARML Team,

Happy Sunday! A quick update with a few time-sensitive reminders.

### First Online Tryout -- Today at 4pm

Our first online tryout is **today (Sunday, April 26), from 4pm to 5pm**. The tryout will be posted on registered students' [ContestDojo](https://contestdojo.com/) accounts at 4pm. Please start no later than 4:10pm to ensure you have the full allotted time. The format of the tryout will be similar to an ARML individual round (5 pairs of problems, 10 minutes per pair).

Please treat the tryout as you would any contest -- it should represent your own work, and you should not use outside resources or calculators.

### Guest Access and Issues

If you are not yet registered but would like to participate in today's tryout, please email {{ email() }} as soon as possible with the email address associated with your ContestDojo student account, and we can set up guest access. If you run into any issues accessing the tryout, reach out to us right away so we can help before the window closes.

We are aware that some emails to this address are bouncing -- if you cannot reach us, contact me directly at {{ email(name="moor") }}.

### Registration and Payment Deadline -- Tomorrow

The deadline to register and pay for ARML is **tomorrow, Monday, April 27**. To reserve your spot on the team, you must complete the [Information Sheet] and pay the contest fee ($410) on [ContestDojo] by end of day tomorrow. See [our website](/join/) for details.

If you've already filled out the Information Sheet but haven't yet paid, please take care of that before the deadline.

### More Tryouts This Week

If you can't make today's tryout, or want additional opportunities, we have three more coming up:

- **Tryout 2:** Tuesday, April 28, 6pm-7pm
- **Tryout 3:** Thursday, April 30, 7pm-8pm
- **Tryout 4:** Saturday, May 2, 11am-noon

All tryouts are online via [ContestDojo]. We encourage anyone interested in elite team selection to complete at least two tryouts (including ARML Local). See our [Practices and Tryouts](/practices/) page for full details.

### T-Shirt Design Deadline -- April 30

T-shirt designs are due by **this Thursday, April 30**. Designs must be in a vector-graphics format (.svg or .ai) with a four-color ink limit between the front and back. See [this post](/news/season-2026/shirt-design/) for the full rules, and send your submissions to {{ email() }}.

Good luck on the tryout today!

–Moor and the rest of the SFBA / NorCal coaching staff
