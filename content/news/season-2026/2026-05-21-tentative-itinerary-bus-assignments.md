### Tentative Bus Assignments

Based on the information that we have available, we have assigned students to
bus pickup locations. Please see the end of this email for an updated roster
and find your assigned pickup location. If you'd like to board the bus at a
different pickup location, please reply to this email and let us know. We will
try to accommodate such requests within the constraints of capacity limits for
our two buses.

### Alternate Transportation

A number of you have asked about traveling independently with parents to ARML.
In order to maintain supervision during the trip, we require that any student
traveling independently be dropped off and picked up at UNR at the following
designated times:
- Friday May 29: Drop-off between 1:30pm and 2:00pm.
- Saturday May 30: Pickup between 3:15pm and 3:30pm.

The exact drop-off and pickup location at UNR has not yet been finalized, and
will be included in the final itinerary.

If we've already recorded your intention to travel independently, your travel
in the table below should be listed as "parent". If you're assigned to the bus
but plan to drive with parents instead, please reply to this email and let us
know, so that we're not looking for you when loading the buses.
