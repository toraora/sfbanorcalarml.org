The 2026 ARML Competition will be held from Friday, May 29 to Saturday, May 30, 2026 at the University of Nevada Reno. 
We anticipate opening registration in late March, with a registration deadline in late April.

Our tentative season schedule is as follows:

| Date           | Event |
| -------------- | ------- |
| late March         | Registration Opens |
| April 19 (Sunday)  | ARML Local (in-person) |
| late April | Registration Deadline |
| late April | Online Tryouts |
| May 3 (Sunday)    | First Bay Area Practice |
| May 9 (Saturday)  | Second Bay Area Practice |
| May 16 (Saturday) | Third Bay Area Practice |
| May 29 -- 30 (Fri -- Sat) | ARML in Reno |

and we thank them for their generous support of our team.
