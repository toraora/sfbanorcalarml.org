Dear ARML Team,

We hope those of you who came out to SMT and ARML Local last weekend had a great time! This is a busy week with several important dates coming up, so please read through this update carefully and email us at {{ email() }} if you have any questions.

We are aware that some emails to this address are bouncing -- if you cannot reach us, contact me directly at {{ email(name="moor") }}.

### First Online Tryout

Our first online tryout will take place this **Sunday, April 26, from 4pm to 5pm**. The tryout will be posted on registered students' [ContestDojo](https://contestdojo.com/) accounts at 4pm. Please start no later than 4:10pm to ensure you have the full allotted time.

If you haven't registered yet but still want to participate, email {{ email() }} ahead of time with the email address associated with your ContestDojo student account, and we can give you guest access.

As a reminder, we have four online tryout opportunities this season (April 26, 28, 30, and May 2), and we encourage anyone interested in elite team selection to complete at least two (or one in addition to ARML Local). For full details on all tryout times and logistics, see our [Practices and Tryouts](/practices/) page.

Please treat the tryout as you would any contest. The tryout should represent your own work, and you should not use outside resources or calculators.

### Registration and Payment Deadline

The deadline to register and pay for ARML is this coming **Monday, April 27**. In order to reserve your spot on the team, you must complete the [Information Sheet] and pay the contest fee ($410) on [ContestDojo] by this date. Please see [our website](/join/) for details.

If you've already filled out the Information Sheet but haven't yet paid, please do so before the deadline. For our planning, the sooner you can get this done, the better.

### Parent and Coach Volunteers

If you are a parent or coach who is interested in coming to Reno as a volunteer this year, please fill out the [Information Sheet](https://forms.gle/oqBLJ7P33sA5yW1x6) as soon as possible. ARML requires that we finalize our travel roster soon, including volunteers. Parent and coach volunteers will be asked to assist throughout the trip, and will be provided with a dorm room at UNR.

We will soon send out a second form for coaches, parents, and volunteers to fill out in order to commit to joining the trip to Reno.

### T-Shirt Designs

A reminder that t-shirt designs are due by **April 30**. We'll put submitted designs to a vote in early May. Designs must be in a vector-graphics format (.svg or .ai), and there's a four-color ink limit between the front and back. See [this post](/news/season-2026/shirt-design/) for the full set of rules.

If you'd like help with your design, send it in soon so we have time to give feedback before the deadline.

### Sacramento / Davis

If you live in the Sacramento / Davis area, there are separate weekly practices held at UC Davis. For more information, please visit the [Sacramento page](/sac/) or contact {{ email(name="sac") }}.

### Action Items

Here's what you should be doing right now:

- Participate in our first online tryout this Sunday, April 26, at 4pm!

- Register for ARML by April 27: fill out the [Information Sheet] and pay on [ContestDojo].

- Tell your friends to sign up too -- Monday is the deadline.

- If you want to submit a t-shirt design, get it in by April 30.

We're looking forward to seeing everyone at the tryout this weekend!

–Moor and the rest of the SFBA / NorCal coaching staff
